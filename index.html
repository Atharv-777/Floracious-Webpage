Welcome To Floracious

Let us introduce you to this app.

This Application is for taking care of your plants. It's like your guide for you about your plants. We know that there are many peeps who like to do gardening but due to their hectic schedule, they are unable to do so. So, here comes this app that will remind you to look after your plants(your mini garden at home) like it will remind you to water plants, it will show you a proper place where there's sufficient light at your home. Check out the instructions given below how to use this app.

Instructions

This is the Home Screen of this app, which contains 4 options - AR, option for setting reminder(for watering the plants), option to search information about particular plant and a compass to know postion of the sun.

This is the Search Screen of this app. It includes information about some plants and it also contains search bar which search for particular plant.

This is the Set Reminder Screen of this app. This section can be used for setting reminder for watering plant. User have to enter time in "hour:minutes" format after which app will give reminder
